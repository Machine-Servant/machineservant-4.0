Welcome to part five of "Building a GatsbyJS Blog!" In this post, we'll be taking our blog to the next level by discussing search engine optimization (SEO) techniques. SEO is essential for any website, and it's no different for our GatsbyJS blog. By optimizing our blog for search engines, we can increase our visibility, drive traffic to our site, and grow our audience.

In this post, we'll be covering the basics of SEO for our GatsbyJS blog. We'll start by discussing the importance of metadata, and we'll show you how to add metadata to your blog. We'll also cover sitemaps, robots.txt files, and other essential tools for optimizing your blog's SEO.

Finally, we'll take a look at Open Graph (OG) tags, which are crucial for optimizing your blog's social media presence. With these techniques, you'll be able to make your blog more visible, more accessible, and more successful. So let's dive into the world of SEO and take our GatsbyJS blog to the next level!
